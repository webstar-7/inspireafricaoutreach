    About

    INSPIRE AFRICA OUTREACH is a Non Governmental Organization (NGO) registered at the registrar general's department of Ghana in the year 2014.

    Vision

    To have a healthier tomorrow.

    Mission

  1. To improve quality of life for all.
  2. To educate people to choose a healthy lifestle.

    3. Since our inception we have been promoting PREVENTIVE HEALTH CARE, WOMEN AND CHILDREN EMPOWERMENT, CAREER EMPOWERMENT and ICT EDUCATION through outreach programs.

    We have been conducting health intervention programs in schools, churches and communities, some of which are Islamic communities where we educate people to maintain a healthy lifestyle and screen them to determine if there is any condition that require medical attention. We also help equip mothers and girls in vulnurable communities with skills.
